
#  LibreOffice Writer – Practical Training Scenario & Full Guide

---

##  Training Scenario (Real-World Based)

### **Scenario Title:**

*Preparing an Official University Circular*

### **Situation:**

You are an **academic administrative officer**.
The **Dean** has asked you to prepare an **official circular** to inform staff about:

> A **Faculty Staff Meeting** scheduled for next month.

The document must:

* Look **professional**
* Follow **official formatting**
* Include **logo, headings, table, bullet points, and signature**
* Be **saved as PDF** and **printed**

---

##  Expected Final Output

A **one-page official circular** containing:

* University header
* Circular number & date
* Subject line
* Meeting details (table)
* Agenda (bulleted list)
* Signature block
* Footer with page number

---

#  FULL STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

---

##  Step 1: Open LibreOffice Writer

1. Click **Start Menu**
2. Open **LibreOffice**
3. Select **Writer**
4. Choose **Blank Document**

---

##  Step 2: Set Page Layout (Very Important)

<img src="https://github.com/LEARN-LK/libreoffice/blob/main/0-learn-Page-Layout.png"  style="max-width: 100%;width: 500px;">

1. Go to **Format → Page Style**
2. Set:

   * **Page:** A4
   * **Orientation:** Portrait
   * **Margins:**

     * Top: 2.5 cm
     * Bottom: 2.5 cm
     * Left: 3 cm
     * Right: 2.5 cm
3. Click **OK**

---

##  Step 3: Insert University Header

### 3  add Header

<img src="https://github.com/LEARN-LK/libreoffice/blob/main/0-add-Header.png"  style="max-width: 100%;width: 500px;">

1. **Insert** > **Header and Footer** > **Header** > **Default Page Style**

### 3.1 Insert Logo

<img src="https://github.com/LEARN-LK/libreoffice/blob/main/0-Insert-Logo.png"  style="max-width: 100%;width: 500px;">

1. Go to **Insert → Image**
2. Select university logo
3. Resize and  **Format** > **align Text** **Center**

### 3.2 Add University Name

Type:

```
UNIVERSITY OF XYZ
Faculty of Science
```

* Select text
* **Font:** Times New Roman
* **Size:** 14 (University), 12 (Faculty)
* **Alignment:** Center
* **Bold**

---

##  Step 4: Insert Horizontal Line

<img src="https://github.com/LEARN-LK/libreoffice/blob/main/0-Insert-Horizontal-Line.png"  style="max-width: 100%;width: 500px;">

1. Place cursor below header
2. Go to **Format → Paragraph → Borders**
3. Enable **Bottom Border**
4. Click **OK**

---

##  Step 5: Add Circular Details

Type:

```
Circular No: FS/2026/01
Date: 15 January 2026
```

* Align **Right**
* Font size **11**

---

##  Step 6: Add Subject Line

Type:

```
Subject: Faculty Staff Meeting – February 2026
```

* **Bold**
* **Underline**
* Align **Left**

---

##  Step 7: Write Body Text

Type:

```
This is to inform all academic and non-academic staff that a faculty staff meeting will be held as per the following details.
```

* Font: Times New Roman
* Size: 12
* Alignment: **Justified**

---

##  Step 8: Insert Table (Meeting Details)

<img src="https://github.com/LEARN-LK/libreoffice/blob/main/0-table.png"  style="max-width: 100%;width: 500px;">

1. Go to **Insert → Table**
2. Columns: **2**
3. Rows: **4**

Fill as follows:

| Detail | Information              |
| ------ | ------------------------ |
| Date   | 20 February 2026         |
| Time   | 10.00 AM – 12.00 PM      |
| Venue  | Faculty Board Room       |
| Chair  | Dean, Faculty of Science |

✔ Make first column **Bold**

---

##  Step 9: Insert Agenda (Bulleted List)

<img src="https://github.com/LEARN-LK/libreoffice/blob/main/0-bullet.png"  style="max-width: 100%;width: 500px;">

Type:

```
Agenda:
```

Press **Enter**, then click **Bullets**

* Confirmation of previous minutes
* Matters arising
* Academic activities
* Any other business

---

##  Step 10: Insert Signature Block

Leave space and type:

```
……………………………………
Dr. A. B. Perera  
Dean  
Faculty of Science
```

* **Align Right**
---

##  Step 11: Insert Footer with Page Number

<img src="https://github.com/LEARN-LK/libreoffice/blob/main/0-footer.png"  style="max-width: 100%;width: 500px;">

1. Go to **Insert →  **Header and Footer** → Footer → Default Page Style**
2. Click in footer
3. Go to **Insert → Field → Page Number**
4. Align **Center**

---

##  Step 12: Spell Check

1. Go to **Tools → Spelling**
2. Correct highlighted words
3. Click **Close**

---

##  Step 13: Save Document

1. **File → Save As**
2. File name:

   ```
   Faculty_Staff_Meeting_Circular_2026.odt
   ```
3. Click **Save**

---

##  Step 14: Export as PDF

1. **File → Export As → Export as PDF**
2. Click **Export**
3. Save file

---

##  Step 15: Print Preview

1. **File → Print Preview**
2. Check formatting
3. **File → Print**
